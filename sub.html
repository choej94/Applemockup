MacBook Pro

The brightest, most colorful MacBook display ever.

The new display in the MacBook Pro is the best ever in a MacBook series. It features brighter LED backlighting and an increased contrast ratio,delivering deeper blacks and righter whites. It's larger pixel aperture and variable refresh rate make it more power effecient than previous generations. This ensures truer-to-life pictures with realistically vivid details,essential for graphic design, color grading, and editing.

For your favorite apps

The Touch Bar can change how you experienve the apps you use every day - including third-party apps. It's a dynamic canvas that developers can use to add a new dimension to existing and future apps.

The most powerful and versatile port ever.

Thunderbolt 3 combines ultra-high bandwidth with the ultra-versatility of the USB-C industry standard to create one revved-up universal port. It integrates data transfer,charging,and video output in a single connector, delivring up to 40 Gbps of throughput for twice the bandwidth of Thunderbolt 2.And both sizes of MacBook Pro are available with four ports,so you can do all that from either side.Existing devices are easily connected with a cable or adapter. And Thundrbolt 3 is reversible,so no matter how you plug in, it's always right side up.
